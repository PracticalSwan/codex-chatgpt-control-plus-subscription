# Consult Patterns

Use GPT-5.6 Sol at High Intelligence through the visible ChatGPT Plus session
for a bounded second opinion, not as independently verified truth.

Good requests name the decision, the approved context, the exact critique or
research question, known constraints, and the desired output format. Ask for
recommendations, assumptions, risks, tradeoffs, evidence to verify, and open
questions.

For a long answer, submit once and retain the thread URL plus pre-submit turn
baselines. Poll compact metadata, read the same thread after completion, and
recover the exact thread after a polling or browser-runtime timeout. Never
duplicate the prompt.
